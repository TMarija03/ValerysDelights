WHY CHOOSE US

VALERIJA VELJANOSKI,
THE CAKE DESIGNER.

I am a food lover, recipe creator and obsessed with a beautifully presented plate. Everything about food excites me, however the one element I truly cherish is the social aspect of gathering around the table with those you love. If you love good looking dessert tables, yummy cakes or cookies and you can not make it by yourself, I will love to do it for you.

Making food has always been a big part of my life growing up in Macedonia where family gatherings are some sort of everyday thing. After graduating and finishing my masters in Criminal Law I met my husband with whom we decided to move and found our place in Melbourne, Australia. After many adventures and with endless support of my loving family in 2020 I took another journey in the cooking world, where I found my true passion. Making delicious desserts that taste as good as they look brings out the creative side in me and fills me with joy.
